Information about Camden,
a reclusive upright ape.


Plotline

Camden was born in 1998 near Staunton, Virginia. He wasted most of his childhood incorrectly partioning laptop hard drives and making short films.

Camden graduated from Fort Defiance High School in 2016 and attended Virginia Tech for one semester that fall. He then transferred to Virginia Commonwealth University in 2017 and studied Cinema and Computer Science for one semester before realizing that art school was a ridiculously stupid waste of money and time. Camden returned to Virginia Tech the following year to pursue a degree in Computaional Modeling and Data Analytics (CMDA) and Philosophy.

He plans to graduate from Virginia Tech in the spring of 2022. No promises though.

Interests

Camden's main interests have been described as "pretentious", "impractical", and "contentious". His intellectual pursuits usually consist of thinking about deep, complex topics (or at least, he believes this to be the case). He's a self-professed technology and computation enthusiast who is interested in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Data Analytics, Ethics, Philosophy, and Politics.

If you can rely on Camden for anything, it's that he will have an opinion, and he will debate you until you have changed your mind, or you have blown your brains out. Though, he may continue to argue with your corpse.

NOTE: Camden is actively attempting to have a more open-mind, and he is trying to be less condescending.
